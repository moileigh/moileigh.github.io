<h2 style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #800080;">The following two poems are from my second book All Is Well. </span><br /><span style="color: #800080;"> I wrote both poems during my cancer journey, the first shortly after diagnosis and the second after I had finished all of my cancer treatments.</span><br /><span style="color: #800080;"> It wasn't until a couple of years ago, when I was preparing for a poetry reading, that I discovered that the second poem shows a beautiful answer to the prayer in the first.</span><br /><span style="color: #800080;"> God doesn't always answer our prayers right away, and He doesn't always give the answer we're hoping for or expect.</span><br /><span style="color: #800080;"> But He does always hear our prayers.</span></h2>
<h1 style="text-align: left;"><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Joy for Fear </strong></span></h1>
<h1 style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>(Written shortly after a cancer diagnosis)</strong></span></h1>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</h2>
<h1 style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;I'm scared today.</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;So much ahead.</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;So much sickness.</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;The journey is too much for me.</strong></span><br /> <br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;But You've made this path for me.</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Help me to walk in it willingly.</strong></span><br /> <br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Give me the grace</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; for each moment</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; You bring me to.</strong></span><br /> <br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Hold my shaking heart.</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Send Your comfort.</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Help me be a comfort</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; to the hurting around me</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; Take my fear</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; and give me Your joy.</strong></span><br /> <br /> <br /> <br />&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <span style="color: #993300;">Joy </span></h1>
<h1 style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #993300;">(Written after a year of cancer treatments)</span></h1>
<h1 style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</h1>
<h1 style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #993300;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;There's a joy in my heart.</span></h1>
<h1 style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #993300;"> &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Through You. In You. Because of You.</span><br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;</strong></span></h1>
<h1 style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Complete, mind-blowing, body-tingling, stabilizing joy.</strong></span><br /> <br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;One of Your many, many gifts to me.</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;</strong></span></h1>
<h1 style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;A joy that doesn't depend on circumstances,</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;what clothes I'm wearing,</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;what day it is.</strong></span><br /> <br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;A joy that only depends on You.</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; comforts my heart in chaos,</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; and cradles my heart snuggly</strong></span><br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;when the world is lost and confused around me.</strong></span><br /> <br /><span style="color: #993300;"><strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;
